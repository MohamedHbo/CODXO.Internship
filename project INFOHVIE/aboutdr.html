About InfoHive

Empowering IT students with comprehensive skill assessments and personalized learning pathways

Our Story

From idea to innovative platform

InfoHive was founded in 2020 by a team of IT educators and industry professionals who recognized a critical gap in the market. While there were plenty of learning resources available for IT students, there was a lack of comprehensive assessment tools that could accurately measure real-world readiness.

Our journey began with a simple question: "How can we help IT students truly understand their strengths and weaknesses before entering the job market?" This question led to the development of our first assessment modules, which quickly gained popularity among computer science departments at universities.

Today, InfoHive has grown into a complete IT readiness platform serving thousands of students across multiple countries, with assessment tools covering all major IT domains.
